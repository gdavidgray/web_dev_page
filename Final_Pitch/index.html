FINAL PROJECT: FISHING SIMULATOR

THE IDEA

  • Make a small fishing mini game.
  • The games mechanics would have simlarities to other games with fishing such as Stardew Valley and Animal Crossing.
  • The game would have an informational aspect to it aswell. After each fish is caught an informational pages about the fish would pop up.

GAME BREAKDOWN

  • Choose type of water to fish in. Different types of water will get you different fish.
  • Cast a line and wait for a fish to bite.
  • When an alert pops up that you have a bite click the screen quickly
  • After clicking once you will have to rapidly click to reel in your catch.
  • Every new fish that is caught can be clicked on to learn more about the species.
